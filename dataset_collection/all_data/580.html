Parents are often confused when it comes to designing and decorating the rooms of their little ones. Whether it's a room for a 4 year old hyperactive little boy, or a daughter that's about to celebrate her sweet sixteenth birhday, it's always a hard thing to do - because, no matter how old they are (except you're designing a nursery, of course), they'll always have their own set of quirky ideas! We may not be able to offer a silver bullet that will help you magically reach a consensus but we can definitely dish out some inspiration!
