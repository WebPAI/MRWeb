Support for Families of International Students
Student > International Students

There is a lot to organise - and with so much information needing to be taken in - we've put together a list of resources to help you understand the support services available to students, what it means to be a successful student at UNSW and how international students can get the most out of their time at UNSW:

  • Visit the International Student Concierge Service where you will find all the information you need to help support your student's journey to Australia
  • Visit International Student Life for advice and information on all aspects of university life at UNSW and in Sydney
  • Explore the different support and development services available at UNSW in Australia to help support your student
  • Check out the Parents and Caregivers information session to hear from Student Support Advisors and Cultural Mentors on all the services available and tips for new students.
  • Student Support Advisors are available for 1:1 consultations with enrolled UNSW students
  • If your student requires Mental Health support whilst studying, visit Mental Health Connect for more information on how and where to seek support.
