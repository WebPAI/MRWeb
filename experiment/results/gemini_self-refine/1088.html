Summer Soirée 2013 - A VIP Event in Support of SummerWorks Theatre Performance Festival

Toronto's historic Campbell House was the site for the pre-SummerWorks Summer Soirée 2013 and Launch Party to raise awareness for the 32nd annual SummerWorks Theatre Performance Festival. Guests had choices of Steam Whistle Pilsner and Mill Street Organic beer along with hors d'oeuvres like smoked Gouda cheese sandwiches from Lakeview Restaurant and Devil's on Horseback (bacon wrapped dates) from Samuel J Moore.

One of the coolest aspects of the Campbell House housed different theatre companies who performed teasers from their upcoming SummerWorks shows or created one-of-a-kind pieces just for the Soirée. Magician Joe Culpepper, (Show and Tell Alexander Bol) delighted guests with his carnation magic trick.

Josh Lazon (Sale of the Century, SummerWorks) made his return as emcee and DJ/MC, which allows him to dream through the microphone and nightmares to be caught in the web. In the piece, Four Walls pushes guests to make real-life status/smart-phone updates. Radio Celebrity Sook-Yin Lee reads Not You Who Lee Forgot/ I Know with Adam Litovitz, Max Chandler, Stephanie Southmayd and Alice Lundy, encouraged the crowd to give them a poem by creating poems using only four words. The witty 4-word poems were then sold for $10 or auctioned off to the highest bidder, much to the amusement of the guests.

One highlight was the comedic excerpt from Paradises Lost which follows the 200 year journey of the starring Discovery Channel's moose in a new uninhibited planet, Bluemouth and a gang of strip poker players. Participants had to reveal a truth if they rocked their lives. And, some did come off clothes.

After the VIP event, doors were opened to the general public for the Launch Party with more tasty treats, live performances and music.

The festival runs from August 8-18 and a complete schedule of performances can be found at http://summerworks.ca
