2013 Food Elves Press Release

The extraordinary effort known as Franklin's Food Elves swarm our canvassing neighborhoods to remind Franklinites about the "10 Days of Giving" campaign. Food Elves will be collecting non-perishable food items in their own neighborhoods or at local participating businesses from December 1st through December 10th. The Elves will be collecting on Saturday, December 7th and Saturday, December 14.

In keeping those in need in the forefront, this is a great way to demonstrate our caring community coming together to make sure that no one goes without during the holiday season. The Food Elves have been collecting for the Franklin community since 2009 and have collected over 10,000 pounds of food each year. Each Elf has their own collection box and the collected items are then donated to the Franklin Food Pantry. There are over 250 Franklin families served by the Franklin Food Pantry, so every bit helps.

This marks the third year that Dean Bank has graciously agreed to sponsor the "10 Days of Giving" campaign with a generous match up to the first $5,000 worth of donations. Dean Bank is a true example of a local business that gives back to the community. Please visit their website at www.deanbank.com or stop by their office at 191 East Central St.

It's a busy few weeks and weekends working with this food elves to make donating easy and convenient. The Elves appreciate the help of our local businesses who offer drop off points. Please help us to support our neighbors in need and remember "every little bit helps".
