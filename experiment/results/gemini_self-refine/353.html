Description

Nestling in a pretty wooded valley on the edge of Exmoor, this small family run park sits on the scenic tourist route between Taunton and Barnstaple. An ideal centre for exploring Exmoor and the Quantock Hills - Superb cliffs, walking and fossil hunting (10 miles) ♦ Wells - Cathedral, medieval streets and market place (25 miles) ♦ Wookey Hole - Visit the spectacular caves, guided tours at Wookey Hole and great water gardens. Exmoor truly offers you the opportunity to get back to nature. For the less active, there are horse riding, walking, canoeing and fishing nearby. Why not tour the area visiting historic Bath, the cathedral city of Wells, Glastonbury, and Longleat Safari Park all providing a great day out for family friendly beaches, traditional seaside amusements and water sports. Minehead and Burnham-on-Sea are within an easy distance from Exmoor Gate. Exmoor Gate lodges are set in a hillside location.
