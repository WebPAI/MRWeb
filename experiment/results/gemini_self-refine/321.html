How to stink up the road to a Victory Royale with Fortnite's stink bomb

Features
By Aron Garst published 14 September 2018

No need to rely on just grenades and timed explosives if you've got this smelly little beauty.

The stink bomb has been the odd man out in Fortnite's lot of portable explosives. Many see the regular grenades, clingers, and timed explosives as far more effective ways to blow away the competition. But have no doubt, the stink bomb can be incredibly useful in short range, mid range, and long range scenarios throughout Fortnite with its deadly assault on the senses. With the aid of our Fortnite stink bomb guide it'll be a difficult item to pass up, even if it doesn't smell great.

The smelly explosive is a common pickup that can be found in chests or on the open. It's a little bottle with green substance in it. That bottle is held and thrown similar to other items. It has a small area of effect after exploding, and it deals five damage per second for nine total seconds. That adds up to a total of 90 damage if the opponent takes the brunt of the attack, just shy of knocking out a full health bar.

There are a number of strategies that the stink bomb can support, even more so than some of the other explosives since its effects linger longer than the typical grenade.
