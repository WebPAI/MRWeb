Claude Bachand on Business of Supply

In the House of Commons on March 10th, 2008. See this statement in context.

Claude Bachand

Saint-Jean, QC

Mr. Speaker, I have the list of witnesses my colleague is referring to. There was Rémi Landry, who often appears on Radio-Canada as a defence commentator; Ms. Peterson and Ms. Banerjee. These people are adding their voices to say that the picture in Afghanistan is not as rosy as we are led to believe. The Bloc Québécois has the most responsible, most balanced solution, as we will demonstrate on Thursday evening.
