Two new publications coming out

Posted on July 17, 2014

Hello! I have two new pieces coming out shortly, one with Forklift Ohio. The latter piece, “Shevaun Looks for a Man” I read at a reading of Bennington Alumni in New York, and one of the editors of the journal was in the audience– she liked the piece and asked me to submit, for which I am grateful because it is really fun, and when it is up at the journal I will post a video of myself reading it be performance based.

I also have been taking the University of Iowa MOOC: How Writers Write Poetry classes, and really much enjoying it! There is still time to enroll and access the videos–I recommend this course. If you’re looking for any of my submissions under sbrannig, and would love your feedback. I am learning a lot in the course, as well as the accomplished poets who have taken the time to film videos for us.
