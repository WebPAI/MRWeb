Sarhan et al now in press at JBC

Congratulations and thanks to Naser for his hard work over the past year. The fruit of his labor (his original manuscript) is now in press at Journal of Biological Chemistry. Which no matter how you slice it, is a pretty cool journal. The manuscript describes the results from our search for potential small-molecule modulators, mutations and assays to do that at a "voltage sensor" molecule called sodium channels in the cardiac sodium channel. it is one more reason we are excited that Naser, who with the Van Epsen, started the project by setting up the biochemistry in the newly founded lab. Thanks to Dr. Van Epsen for the expert assistance, advice and encouragement. Here's to many, many more.
