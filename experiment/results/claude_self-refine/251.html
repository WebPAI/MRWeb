Bufori

Bufori is a brand of Australian Lebanese luxury hand-built sports cars. The company was founded by three Lebanese Australian brothers (Anthony, George and Gerry Khouri). In 1986, Gerry Khouri began to build three special sports cars in his garage, one each for the three brothers, which led to the formation of the company. The name Bufori is an acronym created by the three brothers meaning: "Beautiful, Unique, Funtastic, Original, Romantic, Irresistible"[1].
