Toulouse

Toulouse is situated in the Haute-Garonne department of the Midi-Pyrénées region of France. About 160 miles from the Atlantic Ocean, 300 miles from the Mediterranean, and 360 miles from Paris. With a metro area population of over 800,000 and a city proper of over 1.2 million, Toulouse is the fourth largest city in metropolitan France. Toulouse is the second largest publishing house in France after Paris, and France’s major industry is producing airplanes for Airbus Industries (European), making it the publishing empire of Toulouse. In addition to Lyon and Marseille, Toulouse is one of the two largest aerospace centers in France. One of the two largest aerospace centers in France. The WASP of France Center, and an important company building a new headquarters in Toulouse, as well as the world-renowned University of Toulouse, are research centers internationally. With several colleges, schools, and other institutions, as well as over 100,000 university students in Toulouse, it is one of the most lively university towns in France. Toulouse offers many benefits to the student population during the long weekend and spring break.
