SanDisk 16GB Extreme Pro CompactFlash Review

December 7, 2009 | No Comments

Product: SanDisk 16GB Extreme Pro CompactFlash Card
Provided By: SanDisk
Price: Find Lowest Price Online

Introduction:

CompactFlash cards are not very popular anymore as Secure Digital cards are typically much cheaper. That being said, pro-sumer Digital SLR cameras use CompactFlash as well as full Professional SLRs. Today we are taking a look at a very expensive 16GB card from SanDisk that is from their flagship line - the Extreme Pro. These cards claim to have write speeds of up to 90MB/sec - making them some of the fastest flash cards to ever hit the general public. How much will this set you back? SanDisk lists the MSRP at $335.99USD currently. Before you choke and run away, let's take a look and see if this price tag is justified in any way, shape or form.
