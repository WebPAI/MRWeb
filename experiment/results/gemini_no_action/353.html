Description

Nestling in a pretty wooded valley on the edge of Exmoor, this small family run park sits on the scenic tourist route between Taunton and Bampton. At Cheddar Gorge, a popular tourist attraction, they are abseiling and caving and if you want an active holiday then Exmoor National Park is a stunning area for horse riding, walking, cycling and fossil hunting.

The local area around Exmoor Gate & Somerset

Fun-filled, high-energy break or relaxing in tranquil surroundings, you'll find it all on your doorstep at Wookey Hole. Visit Weston-super-Mare for traditional seaside amusements and great water sports. Head inland to Somerset's county town of Taunton, with its museums, guided tours, Wookey Hole and great Bath, the cathedral city of Wells, Glastonbury, Lyme Regis and Longleat provides a great day out for all ages.
