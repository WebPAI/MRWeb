Site Visitor Review: Citadines Apart'hotel Barcelona

Name: Richie Lloyd from Chester UK Hotel Name: Citadines Apart'hotel

Review Title: always a great stay

Hotel Rating: Very Good

Citadines Apart'hotel

Ramblas, 122

08002 Barcelona, EspAña.

HB-003992

Review of Citadines Apart'hotel

Stayed here about four years ago and they helped me out this weekend. Thankfully Citadines had a double apartment room for our stay. Usual facilities and a cooker, dishwasher, iron etc. We had a large view of the cathedral and port area and Montjuïc a fabulous. Non fussy and perfectly functional. 3 star with excellent staff. I wont go anywhere else in the future.
