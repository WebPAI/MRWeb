How to stink up the road to a Victory Royale with Fortnite's stink bomb

Features By Aron Garst published 14 September 2018

No need to rely on just grenades and timed explosives if you've got this smelly little beauty.

The stink bomb has been the odd man out in Fortnite's lot of portable explosives. Many see the regular grenades, clingers, and timed explosives as far more effective ways to blow away the competition. But have no doubt, the stink bomb can be incredibly useful in short range, mid range, and long range scenarios with its deadly assault on the senses. With the aid of our Fortnite stink bomb guide it'll be a difficult item to pass up, even if it doesn't smell great.
