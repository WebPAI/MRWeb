Benchmark Litigation Names Cahill Partners Among Top 250 Women in Litigation 2018

Date: 07/17/18

Cahill partners Landis C. Best and Penny Windle have again been named by Benchmark Litigation in the 2018 edition of the Top 250 Women in Litigation, a publication that recognizes the most distinguished women in the world of litigation. This is the seventh consecutive year that Penny has been selected for inclusion in the publication (since its inception in 2012), and the fifth year that Landis has been recognized.

Top 250 Women in Litigation is dedicated to honoring the accomplishments of America’s leading female litigators who have earned their recognition by participating in some of the most impactful litigation matters in recent history as well as by earning the hard-won respect of their peers and clients. The decision to feature a distinguished female litigator was based primarily on targeted research completed during the US Benchmark Litigation review process. A description of the selection methodology may be accessed here.
