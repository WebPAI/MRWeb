Sports Academy, Kobe Bryant agree to partnership, and a new name: MAMBA Sports Academy

by Joe Curley

Sports Academy has found "The Mamba Mentality."

Kobe Bryant has reached an official deal with Sports Academy in Thousand Oaks to become a partner in the athletic training facility, which will now be called the MAMBA Sports Academy.

Video Placeholder

The deal was announced Monday via a written release, which included Bryant describing Sports Academy as a place "where we can train the next generation of athletes and provide them with the tools to succeed."
