Our Participants are evolving to Stars

Published: 10 September 2017
Anuj Gala and Carijn Timmermans in the archaeological museum of Kea – October 2014

We are proud that two of our former participants (2014) Anuj Gala from India and Carijn Timmermans from Netherlands, both graduates of the Lighting Design Master Course of the University of Weimar, have been nominated for the Award of the "Best Newcomer" (here). We will be glad to welcome them again to the Aegean island of Kea on the occasion of next workshop as experienced contributors.

Moreover, we are delighted about their contribution as speakers on the forthcoming "PLDC 2017", the most eminent international event about the developments of our profession.
