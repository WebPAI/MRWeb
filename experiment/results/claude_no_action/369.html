TV retailer QVC joins 'Black Friday' frenzy

By TERESA WHITE AP Television Writer
Published: Monday, 2 Feb 2009 6:14 p.m.

NEW YORK - Television retailer QVC has made aggressive plans to keep shoppers watching - instead of mall hopping - on Black Friday, as never it has before.

The leading network for TV shopping promises special deals and a bundle-like deal of new items for early arriving on Thanksgiving night. Program host Dave James plans on working overnight to greet those viewers.
